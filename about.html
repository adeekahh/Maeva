About me

Maëva Le Callonnec is a French Fashion Stylist. She has been immersed in the graphic world since her studies in Applied Arts and fashion design at ESMOD International. She is still continuing her learning at the DONATELLE GODART Wedding Dress House in Paris, while continuing to create and direct her photo projects on the side.

While assisting Emilie Berger (Fashion Stylist) and her team on various photoshoots projects, she realized her taste for photography and scenography.

Light, delicate, elegant, poetic and sustainable are the five tones rooted in Maëva's personality, so everything she undertakes has its origin in these five words. Poetry, piano, watercolour and dyeing are tools that allow her to express her emotions and feelings.
